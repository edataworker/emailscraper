Privacy Policy for EnrichPro

Effective Date:November 20, 2025

Your privacy is our utmost priority. EnrichPro is built on the principle of minimal data collection. Once purchased and installed, the application is designed to function locally on your machine and does not collect, transmit, or store personal user data on an ongoing basis.

This policy explains the very limited circumstances under which data may be accessed or shared.

1. Zero Data Collection Policy

The core functionality of the EnrichPro application operates entirely locally on your device. We fundamentally do not collect, transmit, or store any data generated by your use of the application, including:

  • Scraped Data: We never receive, store, or access the email addresses, URLs, or any resulting data generated by your scraping activity. This data remains solely on your local machine.
  • Usage Metrics: We do not track features used, duration of sessions, launch counts, or general usage patterns.
  • Personal Identifiers: We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, device identifiers, or any other personally identifiable information (PII) from the application during normal operation.

2. Internal Data Processing & Geolocation

EnrichPro processes certain data internally on your device, which is never shared with us.

Geolocation Tagging

  • Processing: The application includes a feature for geolocation tagging, which uses an IP address lookup service that runs entirely client-side (on your computer).
  • Non-Transmission: This IP address and resulting geolocation data (city, country) are used *only* to tag the scraped data locally for your convenience. This information is not transmitted to us (the developer) or to any external third-party service.

3. Voluntary Data Submission Exceptions

The only circumstances under which we receive any data are explicitly initiated by the user for support purposes:

A. Purchase Transaction (Third-Party)

  • All purchases and payments for EnrichPro are handled by Gumroad.
  • Gumroad collects transaction data (name, email, payment information) necessary to process the sale and manage your license.
  • We receive only the necessary data (typically your email address and purchase ID) from Gumroad to manage licensing and communication. We recommend reviewing Gumroad's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your financial information.

B. Technical Support & Error Reports

  • If an application error occurs, you will be given the voluntary option to send an error report.
  • The maximum information collected in this scenario is the technical error message (e.g., error code, stack trace, and timestamp).
  • These reports are stripped of all personal identifying information (PII) and are used strictly to diagnose and fix bugs in future updates.
